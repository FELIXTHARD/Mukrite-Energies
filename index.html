Safe Usage Guide

How to Place
Your Cylinder

Your safety is our priority. Every Mukrite Energies cylinder comes with clear usage guidance. Follow these simple rules to use your gas safely at home or at work.

UNBS
Safety Certified
Vertical, Valve Upwards
Always store and use the cylinder upright with the valve facing upwards. Never lay it on its side.
Away from Heat Sources
Keep the cylinder away from any source of heat, open flame, or direct sunlight at all times.
Well-Ventilated Area
Place the cylinder on or above ground level with adequate ventilation and in an easily accessible position.

Who We Are

Uganda's Gas
Supply Specialists

Mukrite Energies was founded in 2016 with a simple mission — to make LPG gas accessible, safe, and affordable for every Ugandan. What started as a small cylinder distribution business in Kampala has grown into one of Uganda's most trusted energy supply companies.

We serve households, restaurants, hotels, schools, factories, and wholesale distributors across all four regions of Uganda. Our team of trained gas-handling professionals ensures every cylinder we supply meets UNBS safety standards.

Today, Mukrite Energies operates from its headquarters in Makindye Boston, Kampala, with distribution networks spanning Central, Eastern, Western, and Northern Uganda. We are proud to be 100% Ugandan-owned and operated.
