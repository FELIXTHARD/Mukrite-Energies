Safe Usage Guide

How to Place
Your Cylinder

Your safety is our priority. Every Mukrite Energies cylinder comes with clear usage guidance. Follow these simple rules to use your gas safely at home or at work.

Vertical, Valve Upwards
Always store and use the cylinder upright with the valve facing upwards. Never lay it on its side.
Away from Heat Sources
Keep the cylinder away from any source of heat, open flame, or direct sunlight at all times.
Well-Ventilated Area
Place the cylinder on or above ground level with adequate ventilation and in an easily accessible position.
